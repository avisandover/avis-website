Lupine Reservation

Features:

Lupine Reservation offers fishing spots along the Shawsheen River.

History:

Lupine Reservation has a plaque near the remaining piers of the Andover Canoe Club, circa 1910. The reservation also offers a view of a beautiful 19th-century stone arch railroad bridge.

Size:

2 acres

Location:

Off of Lupine Road

Parking:

Lupine Road
