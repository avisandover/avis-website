AVIS Annual Dinner

Andover Town House

April 11, 2023

6 PM

Annual Dinner


The Andover Village Improvement Society will hold its Annual Meeting at the Andover Town House on Tuesday, April 11, 2023 beginning at 6 PM. Cocktails and self-serve appetizers start at 6 PM with a cash bar followed by a buffet style dinner at 6:30.


The Annual Meeting Agenda will begin at approximately 7 PM. Following the annual meeting Agenda will be speaker Mr. Jameson French. Mr. French is the Immediate Past-President of the Land Trust Alliance (LTA), President and CEO of Northland Forest Products, and leader of the first Andover Earth Day Activities in 1970, at which time he was a student at Phillips Academy.


The cost to participate in the dinner portion of the event is $50 per person, and payment is required to reserve your seat. Requests to refund or transfer tickets must be made prior to March 23, 2023. After this date, all tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.
