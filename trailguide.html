Andover trailsguide_5th_edition Guide

The Andover Trails Guide, 5th edition, describes the major reservations in Andover, including AVIS reservations, town-owned land, state land and Trustees of Reservations land. In addition to trail maps, the Trails Guide contains brief descriptions of each reservation and indicates where to park.

The Trails Guide is available from:

  • Andover Bookstore, 74 Main Street, Andover
  • Andover Center for History and Culture, 97 Main Street, Andover
  • Bruckmann's True Value Hardware, 179 South Broadway, Lawrence
  • Rocky's Ace Hardware, 50 Peters Street, North Andover
  • Whole Foods, 40 Railroad Street, Andover
  • Town Market, 429 South Main Street, Andover
  • Butcher Boy, 1077 Osgood Street, North Andover
  • Barron's Country Store, 429 Lowell Street, Andover
