Annual Dinner


We look forward to celebrating the Andover Village Improvement Society with you at the 2023 Annual Meeting and Dinner. The event will include a cash-bar cocktail hour, buffet dinner, an address from the AVIS board of Trustees and a talk.


The cost to participate in the dinner portion of the event is $65 per person, and payment is required to reserve your seat. Requests to refund or transfer tickets must be made prior to March 25, 2019. After this date, all tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.
