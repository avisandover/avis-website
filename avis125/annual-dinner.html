AVIS Annual Dinner

The AVIS 125th Anniversary Annual Meeting and Dinner

The Andover Country Club

April 10, 2019

Annual Dinner


We look forward to celebrating the 125th anniversary of the Andover Village Improvement Society with you at the 2019 Annual Meeting and Dinner. The event will include a cash-bar cocktail hour, buffet dinner, an address from from the AVIS board of Trustees and a talk.


As always, AVIS members are invited to join us for the speaking portion of the event (beginning at approximately 7:30PM) free of charge.


The cost to participate in the dinner portion of the event is $75 per person, and payment is required to reserve your seat. Requests to refund or transfer tickets must be made prior to March 25, 2019. After this date, all tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.

Date: 04/10/2019 (Wed.)
Time: 6:00am - 9:30pm EST
Location: Andover Country Club
