Annual Meeting


We look forward to your participation in the virtual Andover Village Improvement Society 2021 Annual Meeting to take place on Tuesday, March 9, 2021 from 7:30-8:30PM.

AVIS president, John Hess, will lead the meeting, which will provide information on the activities and accomplishments of AVIS over the last year, including its care for more than 25 Andover reservations. AVIS renewing trustees will also be elected during the meeting.

AVIS is thrilled to announce several special presentations. This will include short films about Boy Scout projects on AVIS reservations and a featured talk on the Turtle Mound focusing on research conducted by historian and author Jim Gage.

To cap off the evening, John will offer up a special virtual toast. Please join us in toasting with your favorite beverage. For those interested in joining in with beer, please consider purchasing the re-issued “Journey’s End” brewed especially for AVIS by Oak & Iron Brewing.

Check back later to sign up for the event.
