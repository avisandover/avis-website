The Avis Virtual Annual Meeting - 2022

All Members and the public are Invited to attend the 2022 AVIS Annual Meeting


The Andover Village Improvement Society (“AVIS”) will hold its Annual Meeting on Tuesday, April 12, 2022, 7:00-8:30 p.m. via ZOOM. AVIS President, John Hess, will moderate the meeting and share AVIS' accomplishments over the past year, including how AVIS has cared for its 30 reservations. Selected business items will be covered as well as the election by AVIS members of five people to serve three-year terms on the Board of Trustees.

Our special guest speaker is Dr. Ryan Wheeler from the Robert S. Peabody Institute of Archaeology at Phillips Academy. Dr. Wheeler will speak on the “Indigenous History of Andover.”

AVIS will conduct the meeting via ZOOM. Those wishing to gather in-person may attend the meeting at Oak and Iron Brewing Company (www.oakandironbrewing.com) at 18 Red Spring Rd, Andover.
