Peggy Keck Reservation Evolution

This interactive map of the Peggy Keck Reservation shows how AVIS reservations are often assembled by many small acquisitions of property over a period of years. The first map shows mostly open land with the first donation of land in 1963 by Phillips Academy. Each layer shows subsequent acquisitions leading up to the purchase of the eight acre Cavellaro property in 2008. Thus, over a forty-five year period, many people helped make this wonderful reservation what it is today.
