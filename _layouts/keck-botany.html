Keck Reservation: {{ page.category }}

Walter Kittredge, a botanist from Harvard, compiled a list of the plants present at the Keck Reservation
(You may email Walter at kittredg@oeb.harvard.edu)

He categorized the plants into:

  • Ferns
  • Graminoids (grasses, sedges, and rushes)
  • Herbaceous plant that isn't a graminoid
  • Shrubs
  • Trees
  • Vines
